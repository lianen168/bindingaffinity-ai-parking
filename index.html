T-cell receptors must bind to cancer antigens with precise affinity—too low fails, too high causes toxicity. It's the safety dial.

PROTACs & Glues

Protein degraders require a "ternary complex" formation. Success is measured solely by the cooperative binding affinity of the linker.

CRISPR & Gene Editing

Off-target effects happen when Cas9 binds to the wrong DNA. Measuring binding affinity is the only way to ensure patient safety.

Keyword Freq: Very High

Virtual Screening SaaS

In-Vitro Diagnostics
